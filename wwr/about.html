White Water Rafting

At Splash White Water Rafting, we create thrilling, safe river adventures that connect people with nature. Our mission is to deliver unforgettable experiences while supporting the environment and community. Guided by “Adventure with Integrity,” we value safety, fun, and professionalism.

History

Founded in 2010, Splash White Water Rafting began as a small, passionate team of outdoor enthusiasts dedicated to sharing the thrill of river adventures. Over the years, we’ve grown into one of the region’s most trusted rafting outfitters, offering safe, fun, and unforgettable whitewater experiences for families, first-timers, and thrill-seekers alike. With a commitment to safety, nature, and community, Splash continues to make waves in the world of outdoor recreation.
