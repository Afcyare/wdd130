Somalia

Somalia is a country located in the Horn of Africa, bordered by Ethiopia, Kenya, and Djibouti, with a long coastline along the Indian Ocean. It has a rich cultural heritage rooted in Islam, traditional clan structures, and a history of trade and seafaring. Despite facing challenges such as political instability and drought, Somalia is known for its resilient people, vibrant oral poetry, and strong sense of community. The capital city is Mogadishu, and Somali is the official language. Somalia continues to work toward rebuilding its institutions and economy while preserving its deep cultural traditions.
